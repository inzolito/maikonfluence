ME Data Management QuerySQL TablesNotes

ConTyp - Consumable Type

dbo.ConsumableType

Groups parts and materials

Examples: Fuel, Water, Drill Bit, Hammer, Explosive

ConGro - Consumable Groupdbo.ConsumableGroup

Groups the parts and materials to match the Operator's role

Examples: Drill, Blast, General

Consumable - Consumable

dbo.Consumable

  • dbo.ConsumableGroup
  • dbo.ConsumableType

The details of the parts and materials

Never delete an item, instead set Active to No. Deleting data prevents data from appearing in report.

ReaEquTyp - Reason Equipment Type

dbo.ReasonEquipmentType

  • dbo.Reason
  • dbo.Equipment
  • dbo.Consumable

What the Operator is doing to trigger the Consumables options. Matches the Operator's Equipment Type to the Consumables.

Examples:

Delay - Refuel offers Consumable - Fuel
(General to all Groups)

Delay - Bit Change offers Consumables with Bits (specific to the Equipment Type)

Developer Notes

EquipmentTypeReason joined with consumable where equipmentType.code is in equipment codes AND consumable exists AND consumable is active AND equipment type reason AND equipment Reason Data entry style
