Overview

This section contains information specific to the 2020.04 release of MineOperate Foundation Applications

The MineOperate release is targeted for GA in April 2020. 

This version contains the release of the Foundation Apps v4.0 that support Andriod 9 & 10.

This Haul App release is dependent on MineEnterprise v3.6.0

MiApp framework updated to Cordova for Android v8 

https://cordova.apache.org/docs/en/latest/guide/platforms/android/

4.0 Apps are compatible with Android 8, 9 + 10.
