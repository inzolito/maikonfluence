Overview

Pan American Silver (previously Tahoe Resources) selected the MineOperate Foundation solution for their underground operations at Timmins, Ontario, Canada.

As part of the project, a number of new features and product improvements were requested by the customer.



Scope

Within scope

Phase 1 - MineEnterprise + MineOperate Foundation Haul implementation

Phase 2 - Bluetooth Low Emitting (BLE) beacon Equipment location tracking, Haul App auto-location population feature (based on BLE beacon location services), MineEnterprise Beacon administration and live mine map enhancements
