Overview

BHP’s Nickel West operation seeks to utilize a tablet based solution in their loaders on the ROM pad to feed the Crusher, MiPlan has committed to developing such an application called MiBlend which will allow authorized personnel to load up a particular blend and publish that to the app and allow the operator to deplete the blend as they take buckets from each required stockpile.

Source: BHP-264 MiBlend ROM Solution
