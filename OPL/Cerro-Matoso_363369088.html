OP PRO LATAM : Cerro Matoso

Created by JUNIOR Nelson, last modified on oct 21, 2024

The Cerro Matoso mine uses hardware with x900 displays (32-bit - platform 2.26.4) and x900 (64-bit - Masep 3.3.18). According to the information collected, they use an automatic loading cycle, and they only execute manual cycle when the automatic cycle encounters an error. But it is rare!


Spreadsheet containing versions and details of all registered equipment.


Cerro Matoso.xlsx


According to information received about the Cerro Matoso mine, they do not work with FA 2.0 reports, making it impossible to collect data on:

CS-012 Shift Data Compliance

CS-014 Current Shift Dispatcher Summary


They use VIMS for payload and dipper. Evidence follows below:



select equipment_id, time_depart, count (*) from shift_hauls where time_stamp between '2024-07-15 15:00' and '2024-10-21 17:00' group by equipment_id,time_depart having count (*) > 2
