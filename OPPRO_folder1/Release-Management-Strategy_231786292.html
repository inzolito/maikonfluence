Customer Upgrade Plan

  • OP Pro 3.0 Upgrade Strategy
    • Work with regions to categorise customers intoÂ 
      • Early Adoptors
      • Early Majority
      • Late Majority
      • Laggards
    • Develop a prioritised customer upgrade list with tentative upgrade schedule for next 12-24 months for each region
    • Assess the upgrade plan in terms of available PM & PD & Services resources

Customer Upgrade Assessment

  • Onboard Panel Gap Analysis
    • For each customer conduct an onboard panel gap analysis between their existing Truck & Shovel panels V's the new OP Pro 3.0 Web Panels
      • Identify customer screens, features, workflows that have been developed for the customer
      • Asses these gaps and conduct a MoSCoW prioritisation of the customer requirements
      • Assess these requirements (MUST & SHOULD) have and validate whether they will be included in the generic panel build
      • Services and Account Management should work with the customer to assess whether COULD and WILL NOT have requirements can be omitted or achieved by doing things differently or changing their process


  • Onboard Panel Development
    • Generally the process for new panel features will be
      • Functional Requirements and Mock Up (App Eng & PM)
      • UI/UX Design (UI/UX Team)
      • Epic creation (PM)
      • PD Estimate (PO)
      • PD Scheduling (PO/PM)
      • PD & QA
      • FC
      • PR
