MINH: Asset Health

Product Status

RELEASED

Product

HxGN MineOperate Asset Health

Product Manager

Ryan Smith

Concept

Asset Health is designed as a system of micro services, advanced data visualization, and reporting tools that identify machine health trends and foresee unplanned events in real-time, to eliminate equipment down-time and lost revenue.
Product development of Asset Health is the first Mining solution to utilize Hexagon’s Xalt on its development by incorporating EdgeClient.

1.X (2019-2022)

Current Release Version (1.4)

  • Data Collection for RTD
  • Data Visualization
  • Virtual Objects (Sensors and Alarms)
  • Operator/FMS Alerts
  • Alarm Management
  • Web Reporting
  • Standalone support w/limitations
2.X (2023-2026)
  • Full standalone support
  • Desktop UI/UX to web
  • Equipment Health Vitals 
  • Maintenance FMS Integration 
  • Mobile Alarm Alerts (SMS/Email) 
  • 3rd party integrations (oil analysis, planned maintenance schedule, primary maint sys)
  • LV ODB2 integration w/ Safety HW
  • Smart maintenance schedule
  • Failure prediction
  • Mobile App
3.X (2026-2029)
  • TBD
